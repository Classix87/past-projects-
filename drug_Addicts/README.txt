Drug Addiction & Suicide Risk — Descriptive Analytics

A data analysis project looking into the behavioral and psychological patterns that are linked to drug addiction and suicidal behavior. The goal was to understand what actually drives people toward these outcomes by studying the data behind it.

The dataset was put together by combining multiple publicly available sources and merging them into one complete dataset with all the attributes needed for a meaningful analysis — things like stress levels, psychological symptoms, risk scores, age, and behavioral signs.


What the notebook covers


Cleaning and preparing the combined dataset
Finding correlations between features
Visualizing patterns like how smoking, mood, stress, and age relate to drug use and risk


Stack

Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, scikit-learn — runs on Google Colab.
